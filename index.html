My Work

Ray Ban Music Festival Illustration – Branding The poster depicts how Ray Ban wearing Ray Ban glasses can transform the music festival experience, making it more vibrant and immersive.
Ray Ban Urban Explorer – Perspective based advertising The poster captures the essence of urban exploration through the perspective of a common man's view who aspires to see the world and showcasing how the glasses enhance the experience of discovering the city.
Ray Ban Cool – Perspective based advertising The poster captures the essence of coolness even at on a lazy Sunday.
Animal X Object Integration This handmade sketch integrates a a scissor and an owl symbolizing the fusion of nature and human-made objects.
Typography Exploration – Capturing the gore essence through type This type design captures the tone and plot of the movie using just typography, without any imagery. The sharp and jagged edges of the letters evoke a sense of danger, suspense and death. The pattern going down represents death occuring in an order.
Typography Exploration – Capturing the tragic romance essence through type The flowing and elegant curves of the letters evoke a sense of love, passion and tragedy. The sinking effect at the bottom represents the sinking of the Titanic.
Typography Exploration – Capturing the suspense essence through type The use of perspective with a change in colour shows the path of darkness in the movie. The use of not visible but visible letters through the piano tiles represent the plot.
Typography Exploration – Experimental Type Design This type design explores the use of perspective and form to create a unique and dynamic visual language. The letters are designed to look like they are floating in space, with a sense of depth and movement.
